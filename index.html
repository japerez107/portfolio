About Me

My name is Jacqueline but you can call me Jaque for short. I graduated from the University of Hartford in Conneticut, with a Bacherlors in Computer Engineering Technology. I currently working at a hotel as a Guest Service Rep.

I’m a Full Stack Developer who specializes in HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I have always had an interest in the Technology and the ever growing field. I first got interest in Web Development in College when I was working on my Senior Capstone; we created a website for our project that included basic HTML and some JavaScript. It was then that I really started to look into what web development was and how to go about starting my journey into the field. I love that I can be creative on the front-end side and use logic on the back-end to see everything come together.
